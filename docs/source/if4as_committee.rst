ISIF/CSIF IF4AS Committee
=================

.. admonition:: The Special Committee of Information Fusion for Autonomous Systems at CSIF

   Autonomous systems pose unique challenges for information fusion. Information Fusion, as presented by both the International Society of Information Fusion (ISIF: isif.org) and China Society of Information Fusion (CSIF), encompasses theory, techniques and tools conceived and employed for exploiting the synergy in the information acquired from multiple sources (sensor, databases, information gathered by human, etc.) such that the resulting decision or action is in some sense better (qualitatively or quantitatively, in terms of accuracy, robustness, etc.) than would be possible if any of these sources were used individually without such synergy exploitation. From aerospace classic data fusion theory to modern smart sensor fusion vehicle intelligence, information fusion has become the enabler for high level automation. 

   On Nov. 16th 2025, CSIF officially forms a **special committee** on **Information Fusion for Autonomous Systems (IF4AS)**. The IF4AS special committee aims to promote FUSION technologies for industry-level autonomous systems, to advance rapid transition of FUSION technologies from research to applications and to foster international collaborations and cooperation between ISIF and CSIF.
